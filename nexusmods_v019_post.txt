The Hermit v0.1.9 - Community Contributor Update

This is a major update incorporating community contributions and fixing a large number of gameplay bugs. Huge thanks to everyone who has been testing, reporting issues, and contributing directly to the mod.

BUG FIXES

Visual / Scene Fixes:
- Fixed combat softlock (black screen when entering combat) caused by missing scene files in the resource pack
- Fixed The Hermit not appearing in the merchant scene
- Fixed character select portrait still showing placeholder art instead of the updated portrait
- Fixed in-game character icon (top-left corner) showing the old placeholder icon

Card Fixes:
- Fixed Final Canter: changed rarity from Uncommon to Rare (matching STS1), and the card now correctly deals bonus damage per Curse in all piles (+3 base, +4 upgraded) -- thanks metacusa
- Fixed Gestalt upgrade: Vulnerable now correctly reduces from 2 to 1 on upgrade, while Rugged stays at 2 (was incorrectly reducing both) -- thanks steve1316
- Fixed Dead Or Alive: killing an enemy now correctly grants gold (15 base, 25 upgraded) and tracks it via Bounty -- thanks metacusa
- Fixed Luck of the Draw: the card now properly triggers a reshuffle from discard pile when the draw pile runs out mid-effect, instead of stopping early -- thanks Dartblue
- Fixed Flash Powder: base card now correctly removes 2 Strength (was only removing 1). Upgrade now adds Block (5 base, 8 upgraded) instead of increasing Strength loss -- thanks metacusa
- Fixed Cheat: the card now draws top 3 (5 upgraded) cards to hand and lets you choose one to play, instead of acting as a single draw. Dead On correctly chains to the chosen card -- thanks metacusa
- Fixed Coalescence: retain selection now works correctly during card play. Choose up to 2 (3 upgraded) cards from hand to retain this turn -- thanks metacusa
- Fixed Low Profile: bonus Block per debuff on the owner now applies correctly -- thanks Condomknight
- Fixed Spray and Pray: now correctly hits a random enemy per hit (not all enemies), and properly shuffles a Doubt into the draw pile -- thanks berseker2111
- Fixed Cursed Weapon: damage now correctly scales permanently each time you play a Curse -- thanks gigaflairman

Power Fixes:
- Fixed Determination: "Whenever a debuff is applied to you, gain X Strength" now correctly triggers on any debuff application, not just the power's own changes -- thanks metacusa

Card Pool / Reward Fixes:
- Fixed Memento, Snapshot, and other Basic-rarity cards appearing in card rewards and Discovery. Basic cards are now properly filtered from the Hermit card pool -- thanks metacusa

Stability Fixes:
- Fixed DustyTome softlock: added a Harmony patch to prevent NullReferenceException when The Hermit encounters the DustyTome relic (which expects base-game character properties) -- thanks Darv
- Fixed epoch progression crash: added null safety checks to elite and boss epoch tracking to prevent NullReferenceException when progress data is unavailable -- thanks metacusa

---

KNOWN ISSUES

- The Wsdx233 variant of the game uses a different mod loading system and is not currently supported. The mod requires the official Steam version with BaseLib.
- Discovery may generate empty choices if the card pool is fully exhausted after many uses in a single combat. This is rare and non-blocking (you can cancel the selection).
